About Us

We Are TBH

The Blockchain Hub (TBH) is a student-driven technology club dedicated to exploring the potential of Blockchain technology and its impact on the FinTech industry. We operate through distinct wings: PR, Tech, Design, Draft, and Non-Tech, offering students diverse opportunities to engage in both technical and non-technical aspects of this revolutionary field. Our mission is to foster innovation, practical learning, and collaboration, providing members with hands-on experience, industry connections, and a platform for career development.
